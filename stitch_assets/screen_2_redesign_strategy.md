# Money Flow App - Mobile Redesign Plan

## Overview
The goal is to modernize the "Money Flow" application while maintaining its core functionality (tracking income/expenses, categories, accounts, and debts). The new design will lean into a "Modern Fintech" aesthetic: clean lines, purposeful whitespace (even in dark mode), vibrant accent colors for data visualization, and a clear information hierarchy.

## Screens to Generate (Mobile - Portrait)
1.  **Dashboard (Ringkasan)**: High-level overview of balance, recent activity, and AI insights.
2.  **Transactions (Transaksi)**: Clean list of history with intuitive filtering and search.
3.  **Categories (Kategori)**: Grid or list of expense/income types with modern iconography.
4.  **Accounts (Akun/Rekening)**: Card-based view of different funding sources.
5.  **Debts (Hutang & Paylater)**: Progress-tracking view for liabilities.

## Design Principles
- **Modernity**: Use glassmorphism and subtle gradients.
- **Clarity**: High contrast for financial figures.
- **Interactivity**: Floating action buttons (FAB) for quick entry.
- **Responsiveness**: Focus on mobile-first touch targets and bottom navigation.
